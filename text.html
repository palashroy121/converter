Unicode to Bijoy converter is big tool for converting universal platform supported Unicode font to bijoy ANSI font. Someone ask the question why we need such type of conversion, It is easy to say that bijoy is very important for the Bangladeshi people for writing content in print media but they collect information from website like Wikipedia Bangla and those websites content is written in Unicode font like solaimanlipi etc. The question is now those contents are not looking nice when they paste in Microsoft Word or Photoshop. So conversion is very essential here to display bangla as correct nice looking. SutonnyMJ is only one font that is very beautiful to look in printing media like books, magazine, newspaper and publications etc.

unicode to bijoy instruction

  1. For Unicode text fonts convert to Bijoy, click on “unicode to bijoy” [ইউনিকোড থেকে বিজয়] button. Then Unicode text will converted into Bijoy and shown in the down box [bijoy editor box] above.
  2. After converted text fonts Unicode to Bijoy, click on “bijoy text copy” [বিজয়ের লেখা কপি] button to copy it from bijoy editor box.
  3. Click on “unicode text copy” [ইউনিকোডের লেখা কপি] button for text copy from unicode editor box.
  4. If you want to clear all text from both editor box (bijoy and unicode), then click on “clear” [মুছে ফেলুন] button.

How to use unicode to bijoy:

You may have some written text as Unicode or in Avro outside of this page, may be those are in an MS Word document. Now Copy that Unicode text and then paste it into the first box [ইউনিকোড এডিটর বক্স]. of the page. Or you may directly start writing Bangla with Avro Unicode keyboard on that box. Now click to “ Unicode to Bijoy – ইউনিকোড টু বিজয়” green button above. Your Unicode text will be converted into Bijoy and shown in the second box above.

Now your converted Bijoy text is ready to use. Copy the bijoy text from the second box and use it where ever you want to.
